Beef Liver and Onions

Description

Tasty beef liver with carmelized onions

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons of butter
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 1 pound beef liver
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Saute onion, in medium skillet over medium heat, for 5 minutes. Stir in sugar, reducing heat to low, and continue cooking and stirring for 25 to 30 minutes, until onion is brown and tender. Place onions into bowl.
  2. As onions are sauteeing, slice liver into 1/4 to 1/2 inch thick slices.
  3. Cover liver in flour
  4. Place remaining butter in skillet over medium heat. Add and cook liver until browned, 1 1/2 minutes per side.
  5. Serve liver with carmelized onions.

Source: "Liver and Onions"
