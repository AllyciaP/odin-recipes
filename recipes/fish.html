Pan-Seared Tilapia

Description

Tasty tilapia that has been pan-seared

Ingredients

  • 4 (4 ounce) tilapia fillets
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons melted, unsalted butter
      • Optional:
    • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
    • 1 teaspoon chopped fresh parsley
    • 1/2 teaspoon chopped fresh thyme

Steps

  1. Rinse tilapia fillets in cold water, then pat dry with paper towels. Season each fillet side with salt and pepper.
  2. Press fillet into flour in a dish, coating and shaking off any excess.
  3. In large skill, heat olive oil over medium-high heat. Cook tilapia fillets until fish flakes easily, about 4 minutes per side.
  4. Before removing from skillet, brush tilapia fillets with melter butter in the last minute of cooking.
  5. Drizzle fillets with lemon juice and garnish with parsley and thyme.
